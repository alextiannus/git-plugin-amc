# AIDA Engine

AIDA is the most battle-tested copywriting framework. For F&B brands, it maps naturally to how customers discover, consider, and order food. Use it for captions, email subject lines, and ad copy.

---

## When to Use

- Writing promotional captions for new menu items
- Crafting launch announcements for seasonal dishes
- Creating ad copy for delivery platform promotions
- Any content where the goal is to drive immediate orders

## When NOT to Use

- Evergreen brand-building content (use StoryBrand instead)
- Review response copy
- Google Maps business descriptions

---

## Framework

**A — Attention** (1–2 sentences)
Hook the reader instantly. Lead with a bold claim, surprising fact, sensory detail, or question. Make stopping the scroll non-optional.

**I — Interest** (2–3 sentences)
Deepen the hook. Explain *why* this matters to them — context, origin story, technique, or what makes it different. No generic adjectives.

**D — Desire** (2–3 sentences)
Make them want it. Paint the experience: taste, texture, smell, occasion. Connect to an emotion or identity — family, nostalgia, celebration, adventure.

**A — Action** (1 sentence)
One clear next step. Be specific. "Order now" is weak; "Tap the link to get it delivered in 30 min" is strong.

---

## Formula

```
[ATTENTION]
{{bold_hook}} — {{unexpected_fact_or_claim}}

[INTEREST]
{{why_it_matters}} + {{origin_or_technique}}

[DESIRE]
{{sensory_experience}} — {{emotional_connection}}

[ACTION]
{{specific_cta_with_friction_removed}}
```

## Examples

### Example 1 — New Dish Launch (Instagram, EN)

**Input:** Introducing char siu pork belly bao, soft steamed bun, available for delivery this weekend.

**Output:**
> You've never had a bao like this. 🔥
>
> Our char siu pork belly is slow-roasted for 6 hours until the fat renders into silk — then tucked into a hand-folded steamed bun that took us three months to perfect.
>
> One bite and you're in a Hong Kong tea house on a rainy Sunday morning. Warm, rich, a little smoky. The kind of thing you'll think about the next day.
>
> Available this weekend only — order now at the link in bio before they sell out (they always do).

### Example 2 — Seasonal Promotion (Facebook, ZH)

**Input:** 限时推出：麻辣水煮鱼，外卖优惠立减$5。

**Output:**
> 有一种辣，是让你停不下来的那种。
>
> 我们的水煮鱼用了整整27种香料，红汤里藏着花椒的麻、郫县豆瓣的香，还有刚炸好的辣椒油浇上去的那一声"滋——"。
>
> 冷了一天，就该犒劳自己一顿真正过瘾的川味。
>
> 本周外卖立减$5，今晚10点截止。点击下方链接，现在就点。

---

## Notes

- Keep **Attention** under 15 words — shorter stops more scrolls
- In ZH copy, **Desire** can be longer (Chinese readers respond to richer sensory description)
- For TikTok captions, compress AIDA into 3 sentences total; expand for Facebook
- Never start **Action** with "Don't forget to" — it's weak and passive
