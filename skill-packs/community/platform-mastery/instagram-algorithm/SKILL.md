# Instagram Algorithm — F&B Playbook

Instagram uses separate ranking algorithms for Feed, Reels, Stories, and Explore. This skill covers what signals matter most for F&B accounts and how to structure content decisions accordingly.

---

## When to Use

Apply when planning Instagram content format (Carousel vs Reel vs static image vs Story) and when scheduling posts. Use alongside the core `scheduling` and `content-types` skills.

---

## Format Ranking Overview

| Format | Reach potential | Engagement signal | Best for F&B |
|---|---|---|---|
| Reels | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (highest) | Watch time + shares | New dish reveals, behind-the-scenes, trending audio |
| Carousels | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Swipe rate + saves | Step-by-step recipes, multi-dish showcases, "5 reasons" |
| Static image | ⭐⭐⭐ | Likes + comments | Product hero shots, quote cards, announcements |
| Stories | ⭐⭐ (followers only) | Replies + taps forward | Daily specials, polls, team moments |

**Default recommendation for F&B:** Lead with Reels for reach, Carousels for saves, Stories for daily touchpoints.

---

## Key Ranking Signals by Format

### Reels
1. **Watch time (first 3 seconds critical)** — open with the most visually compelling frame
2. **Completion rate** — keep under 30 seconds for food content; 15–22 seconds is the sweet spot
3. **Shares** — content shared to DMs or Stories gets major reach boost
4. **Audio** — trending audio gets ~2× distribution boost; use audio from the "Trending" tab
5. **Text overlay** — add captions; 80%+ of Reels are watched without sound

### Carousels
1. **Swipe rate** — slide 1 must create a reason to swipe (cliffhanger, numbered list, "swipe for the recipe")
2. **Time spent** — Instagram measures total time on the post; 7–10 slides maximises this
3. **Saves** — the highest-value signal for Carousels; write content people want to reference later
4. **Reshares** — informational or recipe carousels get shared more than promotional ones

### Stories
1. **Reply rate** — polls, questions, and sliders generate replies, which boost your Stories priority
2. **Tap-forward rate** — keep slides under 5 seconds of text; viewers tap forward if they can't process quickly
3. **Consistency** — posting to Stories daily keeps you at the front of the Stories bar

## F&B Content Cadence

```
Weekly posting floor (minimum for growth):
  Reels:    2× per week
  Carousels: 2× per week
  Stories:  5–7× per week (daily)
  Static:   1× per week (optional filler)

Optimal posting windows (general):
  Weekdays: 11am–1pm, 7pm–9pm (local time)
  Weekend:  10am–12pm
```

## Hashtag Strategy

- Use 3–5 hashtags (Instagram's own data shows this outperforms 20–30)
- Mix: 1 broad (`#chinesefood`), 2 niche (`#charsiu`, `#dimsumlover`), 1–2 local (`#torontofoodie`)
- Location tag: always add — improves local discovery by ~15%
- Do NOT use banned or overloaded hashtags (check via Instagram search for "Recent" tab activity)

---

## Engagement Triggers

Use at least one per post:

- **Question in caption** — single, easy-to-answer ("Chopsticks or fork?")
- **"Tag a friend"** — only when the content is genuinely shareable, not as a demand
- **Save prompt** — "Save this for your next order" (natural, not forced)
- **Poll/slider in Stories** — easiest way to get a reply signal

---

## Notes

- Algorithm weights shift quarterly; verify these signals are current before major campaigns
- Instagram suppresses posts that use the word "follow" or "like" in overly direct ways
- For ZH audience content on Instagram, English captions still outperform ZH-only for reach on this platform
- Collab posts (tagging another account as co-author) can double reach — use for supplier or neighbourhood collaborations
