## Review Response Guidelines

### Responding to Positive Reviews

**Formula:** Thank + Personalize (mention specific dish if named) + Invite back

**Example:**
> "Thank you so much, [Name]! We're really glad the 麻辣香锅 hit the spot — our kitchen team works hard to get the spice balance right every time. We hope to see you again soon, and let us know what you'd like to try next! 🙏"

**Rules:**
- Never copy-paste the same response — personalize every one
- Mention the specific dish if the reviewer named one
- Keep it warm and genuine, not corporate
- 2-4 sentences maximum

---

### Responding to Negative Reviews

**Formula:** Acknowledge → Apologize (no excuses) → Offer to resolve → Move offline

**Example:**
> "Hi [Name], we're really sorry to hear this — that's not the experience we want anyone to have. Please reach out to us directly at [contact] so we can make it right. Thank you for taking the time to share your feedback."

**Rules:**
- Never be defensive or make excuses
- Never argue with the reviewer — responses are public
- Always offer to resolve offline (never handle disputes publicly)
- Do NOT include personal customer information in the response
- Do NOT offer discounts or free items in review responses (FTC + Google policy violation)
- Flag for Tier 1 escalation if the review mentions: food safety, illness, injury, or contains factually false claims

---

### Review Response — Crisis Trigger

Escalate immediately to Tier 1 ([[operations/owner-approval]]) if:
- ≥5 one-star reviews in 24 hours (pattern suggests coordinated attack or real incident)
- Any review mentions food poisoning, foreign objects in food, or injury
- Any review contains a specific claim that could have legal implications

In crisis: pause all review responses and scheduled posts until owner clears.

## Business Posts

Google Business posts expire after 6 months. Post 2-3x per week to keep the profile active.

**Post types:**
- **What's New** — new menu items, seasonal specials
- **Offer** — deals with explicit start/end dates and terms
- **Event** — any in-store or community event

**Post specs:**
- 100-300 words
- One strong CTA (order now, call now, learn more)
- Include price if promoting a deal (must match current menu)
- Add a photo to every post (food photo, not stock)
- Offers must include: redemption terms, offer end date, any restrictions

**Compliance reminder:** No "Google Guaranteed" or "Google Verified" claims unless officially enrolled in those programs.

## Q&A Management

Monitor the Q&A section weekly. Respond to all questions within 48 hours.

**Common questions to pre-populate (proactively add these as Q&A):**
- "Do you offer delivery?"
- "What are your hours?"
- "Do you have a lunch special?"
- "Is the food halal / vegetarian-friendly?"
- "What's your most popular dish?"

## Google Maps — Specific Rules

- Never offer incentives for reviews (violates Google policy + FTC)
- Never ask employees to post reviews
- Never post fake reviews or coordinate review campaigns
- Negative reviews cannot be deleted — only flag if they violate Google's review policies
- Maintain accurate business information (hours, phone, address) — incorrect info hurts ranking
