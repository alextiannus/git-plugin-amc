## The RedNote Formula

RedNote readers are sophisticated. They want the full picture: taste, texture, price, portion, and your honest opinion. Generic promotional copy fails here.

```
[Opening — personal hook or scene-setting]
真的被这碗面惊艳到了…

[Dish details — specific, sensory, honest]
名字：麻辣香锅 (Málà Xiāngguō)
价格：$16.90
份量：一个人吃刚好，两个人分可以再点个小菜

[Honest assessment — pros and one honest caveat if any]
辣度可以选择，建议第一次点中辣。汤汁拌饭绝了。

[CTA or invitation]
你们试过这个吗？评论区说说你们的必点菜单 👇
```

---

## Content That Works on RedNote

- **个人体验 (Personal experience)** — first-person, specific, honest
- **探店 (Restaurant discovery)** — "我去了一家…" format
- **踩雷避坑 (What to avoid / what to order)** — guides for first-timers
- **比较 (Comparison)** — "外卖 vs 堂食" or "哪道菜最值得点"
- **Deal alerts** — time-sensitive price posts perform very well
- **BTS** — behind-the-scenes kitchen content resonates strongly

---

## Photo Guidelines for RedNote

- 3-9 photos per post (algorithm favors 6-9)
- Photo 1: Hero shot — most appetizing angle, best lighting
- Photos 2-5: Different angles, close-ups, portion comparison, process shots
- Photos 6-9: Ambiance, packaging, any UGC (with consent)
- Do NOT use stock photos — RedNote readers can tell, and it hurts credibility
- Slightly "warm" color grading performs better than cool/desaturated tones

---

## Hashtag Strategy for RedNote

Use specific topic tags, not generic ones:
- ✅ `#中餐外卖` `#麻辣香锅` `#[city]美食` `#唐人街外卖` `#留学生必吃`
- ❌ `#food` `#yummy` `#delicious` (too generic for RedNote's discovery engine)

---

## RedNote-Specific Compliance Rules

- 报备 (Declared sponsorship) required for any paid partnership or KOL collaboration
- Use 蒲公英 (Dandelion) platform for all official influencer deals
- No undisclosed paid reviews — RedNote enforcement is active
- Price accuracy: stated price must match actual menu at time of posting

---

## Language Rules for RedNote

- ZH primary at all times
- Dish names: Chinese first + `(Pinyin, English description)` on first mention
- Do NOT machine-translate from English posts — write natively in ZH
- Tone: personal, conversational, first-person ("我觉得…", "亲测…", "真的好吃")
- Use natural Chinese internet language — 绝了、好吃到哭、OMG、确实好评 are all fine

---

## Audience on RedNote

Primarily Chinese-speaking: overseas Chinese students, expats, Chinese tourists, locals who read ZH.
- Hungry Locals looking for reliable Chinese food recommendations
- Explorers seeking authentic Chinese food experiences
- Both audiences respond well to honest, detailed, personal reviews
