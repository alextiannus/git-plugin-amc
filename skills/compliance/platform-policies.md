## Purpose

Platform-specific rules that go beyond general FDA/FTC compliance.
Each platform has its own content policy. Violations risk account suspension.
This file is updated whenever a platform announces policy changes.

---

## Instagram / Facebook (Meta)

**Branded Content:**
- Branded content tag required when partnering with a creator or influencer
- Paid partnership label must appear in the post if any compensation was given
- Promotional codes in Stories must follow Meta Promotions Policy

**Alcohol:**
- Alcohol content must be age-gated (set audience to 21+ in ad settings)
- For organic posts: acceptable, but must not target minors
- No alcohol promotion targeting features on Instagram Shopping

**Health Claims:**
- Meta will disapprove ads with before/after claims or exaggerated health results
- Organic posts with health claims may be reduced in distribution

**Contests & Giveaways:**
- Must include official rules, eligibility, and prize details
- Cannot require sharing/tagging as a mandatory entry condition (Meta policy)
- Always add: "This promotion is not sponsored, endorsed, or administered by Meta"

## TikTok

**Hard limits — these will get content removed or account flagged:**
- No alcohol promotion (applies to most accounts regardless of age gate)
- No weight-loss claims of any kind
- No medical or health claims
- No tobacco, vaping, or drug-related content
- No misleading "miracle" results for any product

**Paid content:**
- Branded Mission / Spark Ads tagging required for paid amplification
- Must use TikTok Creator Marketplace for official influencer partnerships
- Commercial music license required — use TikTok's Commercial Music Library for business accounts

**Trending audio:**
- Always verify audio is cleared for commercial use before posting on a business account
- TikTok's Commercial Music Library is safe; trending sounds may not be

## Google Business Profile (Google Maps)

**Review policy:**
- Never offer incentives (discounts, free items) in exchange for reviews
  → Google will remove the review AND may penalize the listing
- Never post fake reviews or ask employees to post reviews
- Do not claim "Google Guaranteed" or "Google Verified" unless officially enrolled

**Business posts:**
- Posts expire after 6 months — monitor and refresh
- No misleading information about location, hours, or services
- Event posts require actual event dates; offers require redemption terms

**Review responses:**
- Cannot delete negative reviews (only flag for policy violations)
- Responding to all reviews (positive and negative) is recommended
- Responses are public — never include personal customer information

## RedNote 小红书

**Sponsored content:**
- 报备 (declared sponsorship) is required for any paid review or KOL collaboration
- Use 蒲公英 (Dandelion) platform to route all official KOL partnerships
- Undeclared paid promotions risk account ban and brand reputation damage

**Content:**
- No medical or health claims (same as FDA rules)
- No pyramid scheme or MLM content
- Price accuracy: stated prices must match actual menu

**Engagement:**
- Buying fake comments or followers violates RedNote's terms
- Authentic UGC (user-generated content) reposting requires original poster's permission

---

## YouTube

**Content ID:**
- All music must be licensed — use YouTube Audio Library or licensed tracks
- Customer testimonials must be genuine (no scripted paid reviews without disclosure)

**Advertising:**
- Pre-roll ads on restaurant content: food safety claims must be accurate
- Political content: not relevant for F&B, but avoid any political commentary

**Community Guidelines:**
- No misleading thumbnails — thumbnail must represent actual video content
- No spam in comments or description (excessive unrelated links)
